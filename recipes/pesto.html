Pesto

Description

A beautiful basil pesto, goes wonderfully with pasta or on a chicken panini.

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup toasted pine nuts
  • 2 tablespoons lemon juice
  • 1 small garlic clove
  • 1/4 teaspoon sea salt
  • freshly ground black pepper
  • 2 cups basil leaves
  • 1/4 cup extra-virgin olive oil, more for a smoother pesto
  • 1/4 cup grated parmesan cheese, optional

Steps

  1. Take all ingredients except for the olive oil and put into a blender/food processor, blend on medium until mostly smooth
  2. With the blender still running, slowly add the 1/4 cup of olive oil, this will emulsify the mixture
  3. Voila! time to add your pesto to some delicious noodles!
