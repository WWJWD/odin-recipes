Meatballs

Description

This is my take on(see: to the best of my memory) the way I learned to make meatballs. It's a simple recipe, but most great things are. I suppose I'm a bit fond of these because that restaurant holds some "interesting" memories...

  • ground veal don't cheap out... please
  • ground medium beef
  • ground pork
  • a chunk of parm(the real stuff, dont dare use anything else)
  • italian parsley, I mean did you think you'd use anything else?
  • kosher salt and freshly ground black pepper
  • egg
  • milk... please not skim!
  • garlic that didn't come from a jar
