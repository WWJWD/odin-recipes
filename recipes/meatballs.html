Meatballs

Description

This is my take on(see: to the best of my memory) the way I learned to make meatballs. It's a simple recipe, but most great things are. I suppose I'm a bit fond of these because that restaurant holds some "interesting" memories...

Ingredients

  • 1 lb ground veal don't cheap out... please
  • 1 lb ground medium beef
  • 1 lb ground pork
  • a chunk of parm about half the size of your fist(grated)(the real stuff, dont dare use anything else)
  • 1 bunch of italian parsley(chiffonade), I mean did you think you'd use anything else?
  • kosher salt and freshly ground black pepper
  • 3 eggs
  • 1/2 cup milk... please not skim!
  • 6 cloves of garlic (grated), don't you dare use that shit in a jar

Steps

  1. Combine all ingredients and add salt and pepper to taste
  2. Take mixture and make into 2-3" meatballs
  3. Preheat oven to 400f and bake on sheet w/ parchment paper for 25 minutes
