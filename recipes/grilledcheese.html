Grilled Cheese

Description

Classic comfort food, dip it in ketchup or tomato soup, crowd pleaser for all ages.

Ingredients

  • 4 slices of processed cheese
  • 2 slices of thick white bread
  • 1/8 cup butter (unsalted)

Steps

  1. Heat up a non-stick pan to medium heat
  2. Place half of the butter in the pan and let it melt
  3. Construct the grilled cheese sandwich and place in the pan with butter
  4. Cook until one side is golden brown, flip sandwich and add rest of butter
  5. Cook second side until golden brown, remove from pan, cut and serve with ketchup!
